About Me

I absloutely love what I do and is the main ingredient in my baking


Perhaps we've met before or maybe you are here for the first time, either way, I hope you find something that suits your taste!

My name is Petra and I have always been a foodie! I always enjoyed good food and desserts, often more than I should, pssssst! And of course, a good company to share it with! But I never dreamed that life will bring me down this career path. When I moved to Ireland I worked in a few restaurants and met some great chefs and pastry chefs. Then, the passion in me awoke, and it was loud! I decided to enroll into a culinary school and the rest is history, as they say. I worked as a chef and enjoyed every bit of it, however my love for baking was much stronger. I started to experiment, make cakes, cookies, donuts, cupcakes, you name it, with all kinds of new and interesting ingredients and flavors, and I never stopped. My other love and passion is bread, I truly enjoy seeing that perfect sourdough...yummmm!

After baking bread and cakes for my family and friends for a while, I've seen the demand slowly increasing and I decided that it was time to open my very own bakery. And here we are! Feel free to browse the shop, where I offer my best selling products. Rest assured that all ingredients are fresh and carefully and locally sourced.

If you have an idea for the cake or bread or anything else that you don't see here, send me a message and I will be in touch!

Bon appetit!

Love,
Petra
